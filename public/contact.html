FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions before reaching out? Here are the most common things clients ask us.

How long does it take to design and fabricate an exhibition stand?

A typical exhibition stand takes 10–21 days from final design approval to delivery, depending on size and complexity. For urgent projects we offer fast-track fabrication. We recommend reaching out at least 3–4 weeks before your exhibition date.

Do you provide services outside Ghaziabad and Delhi NCR?

Yes! We serve clients across India — including Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad, Pune, Kolkata, and more. Our logistics team handles transportation and our installation supervisors travel with the stand to ensure flawless setup.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

We need the exhibition name and venue, your stand size (in sqm), a brief about your brand and products, any reference images or design inspirations, and your approximate budget range. The more detail you share, the more accurate our quote will be.

Can we reuse our exhibition stand at multiple events?

Absolutely. We build modular and reusable stand systems that can be reconfigured for different booth sizes and layouts. We also offer post-event storage at our Ghaziabad facility and refurbishment services between exhibitions.

Do you provide 3D design previews before fabrication begins?

Yes, all our projects include detailed 3D visualizations so you can see exactly how your stand will look before we build it. You get unlimited revisions on the design until you are completely satisfied, and only then do we proceed to fabrication.

What is included in your turnkey exhibition service?

Our turnkey service covers concept design, 3D visualization, fabrication, graphic printing, logistics, on-site installation, a dedicated supervisor throughout the exhibition, and post-event dismantling. It is a complete, worry-free solution from start to finish.
